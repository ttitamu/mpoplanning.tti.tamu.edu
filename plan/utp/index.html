Steps

  • Instructions

    FIN submits initial instructions to districts.

  • Responsibilities

    MPO/District/Division responsibilities identified and finalized.

  • Long-Range Projects

    MPO/District selects and schedules long-range projects.

  • Category 2/Metropolitan Area (TMA) Corridor Projects & Category 3/Urban Area (non-TMA) Corridor Projects

    MPO/District identifies Category 2 and Category 3 projects for 15-year window beyond current 10-year UTP.

  • Plan Approval

    MPO approves fiscally constrained plan (funding allocation).

  • Plan Submission

    MPO/District submits financially constrained plan (funding allocation).

  • Draft UTP

    FIN develops draft UTP based on project selections.

  • Draft UTP Submission

    FIN submits draft UTP for public review and comment.

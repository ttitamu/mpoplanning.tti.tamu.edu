Saturation Count Map Delivery Meeting

TxDOT TPP Traffic Section (TPP(T)) coordinates a meeting with the MPO and District to deliver electronic files containing maps of where saturation counts were performed during the last count cycle (typically five years earlier). TPP(T) staff explain how to modify the files to show where new counts are desired by the MPO and District, as well as how to add streets that have been built since the electronic files were updated. After this meeting, MPO and District staff will have a time period during which they may add traffic count sites they would like to have for the upcoming saturation counts. This is an important outreach from TPP(T) to directly involve the MPOs and Districts in this process. MPO planners and GIS staff should attend this meeting.
